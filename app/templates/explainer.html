The Math Behind the Madness

Kelly Criterion - The Formula That Keeps Your Account Alive

Here's the formula I learned about in a random Reddit thread that changed everything: $$K = \frac{p(b-1) - (1-p)}{b-1}$$ where $p$ is win probability and $b$ is decimal odds. We use Quarter Kelly because I've blown up enough accounts in my testing to know better. Fun fact: this formula was originally used for phone lines, which is pretty wild considering we're using it to get paid. üìû

Market Implied Probability - What the Odds Actually Mean

Converting odds to real probabilities, because someone had to figure this out:
For positive odds: $$P_{implied} = \frac{100}{odds + 100}$$
For negative odds: $$P_{implied} = \frac{|odds|}{|odds| + 100}$$
When these don't match what my code says, that's when we find the good stuff. üéØ
